Google History

Larry Page and Sergey Brin meet at Stanford. (Larry, 22, a U Michigan grad, is considering the school; Sergey, 21, is assigned to show him around.) According to some accounts, they disagree about almost everything during this first meeting. Larry and Sergey, now Stanford computer science grad students, begin collaborating on a search engine called BackRub.

BackRub operates on Stanford servers for more than a year—eventually taking up too much bandwidth to suit the university.
